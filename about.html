Our journey

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Trusken began as a small supplier of building materials with a vision to transform the construction supply industry. What started as a modest operation has grown into a multinational supply chain solutions provider serving clients across multiple continents.

Our growth has been fueled by an unwavering commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction. We've expanded our product offerings to meet the evolving needs of our clients while maintaining the personal touch that set us apart from the beginning.

Our Mission

At Trusken, our mission is to provide reliable, high-quality building materials and industrial supplies that help our clients complete their projects on time and within budget. We aim to be more than just a supplier - we strive to be a trusted partner in our clients' success.

We achieve this through strategic partnerships with top manufacturers, rigorous quality control processes, and a customer-first approach that prioritizes your needs at every step.

Our Values

Integrity: We conduct business with honesty and transparency, building trust with every interaction.

Quality: From sourcing to delivery, we maintain the highest standards for all our products.

Innovation: We continuously seek better solutions to meet our clients' evolving needs.

Sustainability: We're committed to environmentally responsible practices throughout our operations.

Partnership: We view every client relationship as a long-term partnership for mutual success.
